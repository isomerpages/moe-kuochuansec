<h2>Introduction &amp; Background</h2>
<h4><strong>Media Literacy @ KCPSS</strong></h4>
<p>Think Critically. Create Purposefully. Communicate Powerfully.</p>
<p>In today’s media-saturated world, students are constantly consuming information.
At KCPSS, we ensure they do more than just scroll—we teach them to think,
question, and create with purpose.</p>
<p>Our Media Literacy Applied Learning Programme (ALP) equips students with
the ability to access, analyse, evaluate, and create media across platforms—from
print to digital—through authentic, real-world learning experiences.</p>
<h4><strong>From Consumers to Creators</strong></h4>
<p>At KCPSS, students grow along a clear progression:</p>
<p>Passive Consumption → Active Consumption → Purposeful Production</p>
<p><strong>They move from simply receiving information to:</strong>
</p>
<p>🔍 Analysing and questioning media messages</p>
<p>🧠 Applying critical thinking to detect bias and meaning</p>
<p>🎯 Creating purposeful content with clear intent and values</p>
<p>We develop students who are not just media users, but media thinkers and
shapers.</p>
<h4><strong>What Students Will Learn</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Critical Consumers who:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Evaluate information thoughtfully</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Question sources, bias, and intent</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Make informed judgments in a complex media landscape</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Purposeful Producers who:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Communicate ideas clearly and confidently</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Make intentional choices in how messages are crafted</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create meaningful content</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Authentic &amp; Interdisciplinary Learning</strong></h4>
<p>Media Literacy at KCPSS is a whole-school approach, helping students to
experience learning that is relevant, applied, and meaningful.</p>
<p>Students will:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Apply skills across disciplines (e.g. English, Humanities, Art)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Engage in real-world tasks and problem-solving</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>See how learning is connected and transferable across contexts</p>
</li>
</ul>
