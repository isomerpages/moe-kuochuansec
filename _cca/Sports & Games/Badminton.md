<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>KCPSS Badminton CCA aims to :</p>
<p>Develop each player to exhibit a culture of excellence who are passionate
about the game and embrace our schoolâ€™s GRACE values, with purpose and
perseverance.</p>
<p>Groom a true sportsman who shows respect and fairness towards opposing
player.</p>
<h3>Area of Focus</h3>
<p>KCPSS Badminton CCA believes in providing each player equal opportunity
to learn and grow at their own pace. It is important for the players to
strive to develop themselves both in the physical and cognitive domains,
constantly challenging themselves to level up their skills competency,
learning the rules and strategies of the game.</p>
<p>Sportsmanship and character development are emphasised during the training
sessions, inculcating positive attitude and self discipline in line with
the Olympic values.</p>
<p>The CCA also provides Leadership development where the players are given
leadership opportunities in taking up the various leadership roles, empowering
our athletes to take ownership in leading and building a cohesive and responsible
team.</p>
