<h2>Badminton</h2>

<h3>Vision</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:35%;margin-right:15px;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/The%20Kuo%20Chuan%20Experience/CCA/Badminton/badminton1.jpg">
</div>
<p>KCPSS Badminton CCA aims to :</p>
<p>Develop each player to exhibit a culture of excellence who are passionate
about the game and embrace our school’s GRACE values, with purpose and
perseverance.</p>
<p>Groom a true sportsman who shows respect and fairness towards opposing
player.</p>
<p></p>
<h3>Area of Focus</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:35%;margin-right:15px;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/The%20Kuo%20Chuan%20Experience/CCA/Badminton/badminton2.jpg">
</div>
<p>KCPSS Badminton CCA believes in providing each player equal opportunity
to learn and grow at their own pace. It is important for the players to
strive to develop themselves both in the physical and cognitive domains,
constantly challenging themselves to level up their skills competency,
learning the rules and strategies of the game.</p>
<p>Sportsmanship and character development are emphasised during the training
sessions, inculcating positive attitude and self discipline in line with
the Olympic values.</p>
<p>The CCA also provides Leadership development where the players are given
leadership opportunities in taking up the various leadership roles, empowering
our athletes to take ownership in leading and building a cohesive and responsible
team.</p>
<p></p>

<p><strong><u>Special Programmes</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Holiday Trainings (March/June/Sep/Oct&amp;Nov)</strong>&nbsp;
<br>To enable the players to continue to keep in touch with the game and to
prepare for South Zone NSG competitions for the ‘B’ and ‘C’ Division Teams.</p>
<p><strong>Friendly Games</strong>&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;
<br>Playing against other schools to gauge players’ level of competency and
build up readiness for the actual competition.</p>
<p><strong>VIA (Values in Action)</strong>
<br>Linesmen duties for South Zone Competitions and National School Games
Finals.</p>
