<h3>Vision</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/The%20Kuo%20Chuan%20Experience/CCA/Infocomm%20Tech%20Club/info-club1.jpg">
</div>
<p>Co-curricular activities takes on a holistic approach in developing every
student's character and provide them with the relevant knowledge, skills
and values that will help them face the challenges of the 21st century.
We believe that through our detailed &amp; relevant ICT training programmes,
students will be equipped to become valued and experienced individuals
who will be able to exercise personal leadership in all aspects of their
life.</p>
<h3>Area of Focus</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/The%20Kuo%20Chuan%20Experience/CCA/Infocomm%20Tech%20Club/Area%20of%20Focus.png">
</div>
<p>The Infocomm CCA aims to nurture aspiring young learners, regardless of
their prior ICT experience, by equipping them with diverse digital and
audio-visual skills. Students will learn to use various software and media
tools, develop proficiency in areas such as video production, sound, and
stage management, and showcase their expertise in national competitions.
Beyond technical skills, the CCA provides platforms for students to experience
success, build confidence, and develop essential social and collaborative
skills outside of the classroom. While we encourage excellence in every
project, equal emphasis is placed on preparing our students with enhanced
21st century competencies, ensuring they are future-ready for both higher
education and the workplace.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Programme Framework</strong>
<br>Our core focus is in&nbsp;<strong>M.E.D.I.A.</strong>
</p>
<p>Students will be able to:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Master</strong>&nbsp;core skills through an engaging and structured
curriculum;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Equip</strong> themselves with knowledge in the use of visual media
so that they can creatively communicate ideas through film, animation and
digital storytelling;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Develop</strong>&nbsp;audio-visual skills that can serve a real-life
purpose;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Illustrate and plan</strong> story concepts effectively through
storyboarding;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Apply</strong> stage crew and production skills such as sound,
lighting, and backstage management to support live events.</p>
</li>
</ol>
